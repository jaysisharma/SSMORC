Messages from Our President

SSMORC Mission

Dear Friends and Colleagues,

As President of the Suryagadhi Sahid Memorial Observatory and Research Center, I am excited to introduce our groundbreaking project in Nepal. Our observatory, featuring a 1.5-meter telescope and advanced technology, will be a beacon for scientific research and education.


We aim to create a global hub for astronomical research and collaboration, inviting scholars and enthusiasts from around the world. Set in the Himalayas, our facility will offer pristine views of the cosmos and contribute to international scientific efforts.

This observatory honors Nepal's heritage and heroes, combining science with history and education with tourism. We envision a future where curiosity and innovation thrive, inspiring visitors and fostering a sense of wonder about the universe.


Scheduled to open by 2028, this project represents a shared vision of progress and unity. Join us in building a center of knowledge and exploration that will shine for generations.

Swaraj Sagar Pradhan
President
Suryagadhi Sahid Memorial Observatory and Research Center (SSMORC)
