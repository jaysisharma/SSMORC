Messages from Our President

SSMORC MISSION

Welcome to the Suryagadhi Sahid Memorial Observatory and Research Center.

As the Director, I, Abhishek Shah, am privileged to spearhead our pursuit of astronomical discovery and education.
Our mission is to foster a deep sense of wonder and understanding of the cosmos. Our center is dedicated to pioneering research and delivering innovative educational programs. We strive to make the mysteries of the universe accessible to all, through state-of-the-art facilities and expert-led initiatives. We invite you to join us in exploring the infinite possibilities of the night sky.

Abhishek Shah, Director
Suryagadhi Sahid Memorial Observatory and Research Center
